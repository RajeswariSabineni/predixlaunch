About Predix

On Sept. 2015 GE has announced the launch of Predix Cloud, the world’s first and only cloud solution designed specifically for industrial data and analytics. Predix is a powerful new software platform developed specifically to connect people, data and machines over the Industrial Internet for better business outcomes. Predix offers a catalog of Services that provide core capabilities required by Industrial Internet applications.

Predix Offerings

Collect key industrial data from your machines and stream it to the Predix cloud.
Leverage services and development tools, so you can focus on solving problems for customers.
Use the Predix cloud to deploy scalable services and end-to-end apps in secure environments.

Built on Cloud Foundry, Predix is optimized for secure connectivity and analytics at scale - in the Cloud and on the Edge
